Quality Timber & Handcrafted Furniture for Your Business

Welcome to MWF Ltd

MWF Ltd is a trusted B2B supplier of quality wood and handcrafted furniture. We specialize in sourcing and supplying timber, poles, hardwoods, and softwoods from up-country suppliers to building companies, furniture dealers, and large-scale wood merchants.

We operate a full-service workshop where we craft a wide range of furniture including beds, sofas, dining sets, office desks, cupboards, and custom orders. With a dedicated team and a fully stocked warehouse and showroom, MWF ensures efficient operations, transparent sales, and timely customer service.

Whether you are looking for premium raw materials or high-quality finished products, Mayondo Wood and Furniture is your go-to partner for excellence in timber and furniture supply.
